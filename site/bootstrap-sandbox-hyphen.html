Abbie's Top Five!

These aren't opions, they're facts.

1. Community- Season 4, Episode 5

One of the funniest episodes of community probably EVER. When Troy and Abed decide to share an apartment, they host a party for the group, which takes on an altered reality as several scenarios play out.

Watch on YouTube

2. Never Have I Ever- Funniest Moments Compilation

If you enjoy seeing a main character constantly embarrassed, this compilation is for you! The story follows Devi Vishwakumar, a 15-year-old Tamil girl living in Sherman Oaks, Los Angeles, who is an overachieving but often self-sabotaging high school sophomore.

Watch on YouTube

3. Bob's Burgers- Is Tina autistic?

Bob's Burgers is a show I rediscover every 6 months... and then I can't stop binging. It always makes me laugh! Gene: "How many toothpicks are on the ground?" Tina: "Uhhh, 100?" Bob: "100?!" Gene: "It's 3."

Watch on YouTube

4. The Owl House- Out of context for four minutes

A animated disney show following Luz Noceda, a self-assured human teenage girl who accidentally finds a portal to a magical world. To this day I still do a hooty impression here and there... LULU!

Watch on YouTube

5. Sex and the City- Funniest Moments

I discovered this show during September of my junior year and now I think I can quote every episode... big is moving to paris!! Sex and the City follows a New York columnist Carrie Bradshaw and her three friends—Miranda, Charlotte, and Samantha—as they navigate life in their 30s.

Watch on YouTube
