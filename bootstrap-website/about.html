About Me

Hi, I’m Abbie Smith, a Virginia Tech graduate with a Bachelor of Arts in Professional and Technical Writing. I also have a minor in Public Health. My background includes writing, editing, social media management, and photography.

Through my work with Her Campus, the Virginia Tech English Department, and BurntCupVT, I’ve developed experience creating content across digital platforms, from magazine articles and social media campaigns to promotional materials and multimedia storytelling.

After graduation, I'll be moving to Tampa, Florida, where I’ll begin working as a Donor Recruitment Representative at LifeSouth Community Blood Center. I’m excited to continue building a career centered around communication, community outreach, and public health engagement while also applying to master’s programs in the future.

In my free time, I enjoy exploring pinterest, taking photos of my cat, and taking long walks by the Duck Pond at Virginia Tech. I’m passionate about using storytelling to connect with audiences and creating meaningful content.
